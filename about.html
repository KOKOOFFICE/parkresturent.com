Who We Are?

Park restaurant

the first time in Chamravattom , park restaurant has brought the Open Kitchen restaurant and Fresh Beans Coffee varieties concept where you can enjoy a variety of dishes each prepared by the expert chef to give you the best dining experience. We provide you with a wide variety of dishes to choose from, each created with the perfection which you can enjoy with your famiiy and friends in a peaceful environment.

Upholding our motto of healthy eats wealthy treats, park restaurant only serves fresh, hygienic, and highest quality food to our valuable customers in a due time. With an open kitchen concept, we are established to fill up the need of a place where quality comes with consistency in terms of both foods and services, and a place where you can meet, relax and have a great time together. Serving more than hundred varieties of food made from fresh beans, park restaurant is also a great place for the coffee enthusiast. Enjoy the conversation with a wide variety of foods and exotic dishes to choose from, in an environment built with the perfect ambience which can serve you in both your personal and professional needs with park restaurant.
